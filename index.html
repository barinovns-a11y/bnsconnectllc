Services

Engineered networks. Cleanly installed. Tested to spec.

From single drops to full IDF/MDF buildouts — every job is documented, labeled and tested. Below is what we do most often for general contractors, integrators and end customers in South Florida.

Fiber Optic Installation & Splicing

Single-mode and multi-mode fiber pulling, fusion splicing, termination, OTDR and power-meter testing. Backbone, FTTH/FTTB, distribution cabinets and splice closures.

Low-Voltage & Structured Cabling

CAT5e / CAT6 / CAT6A, coax, audio and control cabling. IDF/MDF buildouts, patch panels, racks, cable management, labeling and certification testing.

CCTV & Security Systems

PoE IP cameras, NVR / video recorders, PoE switches, mounting, configuration and remote access. Clean cabling, labeling, photo documentation and commissioning.

Wi-Fi Site Surveys & Access Points

RF site surveys, AP placement and signal coverage planning. Installation, switch provisioning and testing for retail, hospitality and office environments.

Site Surveys & Documentation

Pre-installation walks, BOM preparation, photo reports and as-built documentation for GCs and integrators.

Troubleshooting & Maintenance

VFL / OTDR / power-meter fault location, splice repair, camera and switch diagnostics, restoration of subscriber service.

Subcontracting Crews

Available for overflow work with low-voltage and electrical contractors across Broward, Miami-Dade and Palm Beach counties.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Are you licensed and insured?+

Yes. BNS Connect LLC carries Hiscox General Liability ($1M per occurrence / $2M aggregate) and a Florida Workers' Comp Exemption. Certificate of Insurance available on request.

What certifications does the technician hold?+

BICSI Technician (#424441), FOA Certified Fiber Optic Technician (CFOT), OSHA-10, plus DBPR Electrical Business and Limited Energy exams passed.

Do you work as a subcontractor for GCs and integrators?+

Absolutely — overflow crew work, single-day mobilizations and ongoing support are all welcome. We can issue COIs and supply W-9 on request.

What testing reports do you provide?+

For fiber: OTDR traces and bidirectional power-meter readings. For copper: CAT5e/CAT6 certification reports. Plus photo documentation and as-built notes.

Which languages do you speak on site?+

English, Spanish and Russian. Ideal for South Florida's mixed crews and customers.
